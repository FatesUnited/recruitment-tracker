About the Recruitment Tracker

The Vapor Lock Recruitment Tracker is an internal tool designed to support the recruitment and membership operations of the Vapor Lock alliance. It allows recruiters to track applicants, manage onboarding progress, and maintain a structured record of alliance members.

Recruiting in New Eden requires coordination between multiple recruiters, interviewers, and onboarding officers. This application provides a centralized system where recruiters can document interviews, record ESI checks, track member status changes, and maintain notes about each pilot.

Each member record contains key operational details including recruitment status, corporation assignment, timezone, onboarding progress, and internal notes. Recruiters can also collaborate through a shared comment thread on each member profile to provide updates and observations during the recruitment pipeline.

The goal of this system is to simplify alliance administration and ensure that recruitment decisions are informed, transparent, and well documented.

Built for the Vapor Lock alliance operating in the EVE Online universe.
